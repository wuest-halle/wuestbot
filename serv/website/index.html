R O U T I N E S

WUEST is looking for individuals and groups to be a part of the inaugural edition of ROUTINES. This week-long exhibition will be spread over different locations throughout the city of Halle/Saale, Germany. Everyday life is driven by numerous guidelines, partly self-imposed and of various kinds in individual ways. This might be more true now than ever. In 2020 it became clear that circumstances may change quickly and significantly so ROUTINES need to be adapted or given up on. What we are looking for is a creative conversation with and about the term to explore its layers of meaning and context.
