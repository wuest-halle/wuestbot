R O U T I N E S » MAY 08 - JUNE 02 - HR. FLEISCHER KIOSK - REILECK

Sven Bergelt - THE INNER CIRCLE

»The Inner Circle« shows the artist walking in a rectangular shape on a field. Over and over, one hour a day for 30 days straight. The monotonous and repetitive action results in a trail, which becomes visible more and more, indicating that time has passed.
After studying media arts in Kiel and Leipzig, Sven teaches at The University for Fine Arts in Leipzig. He is interested in remembrance, society and how they get interpreted in public space. More info: svenbergelt.de



John Horton - PERIPHERY

Periphery (I-IV) is an auditive reaction parallel to »The Inner Circle« using processed field recordings. Starting with sounds collected in the countryside in its initial version, the piece develops during the exhibition itself, gradually layering and manipulating recordings made regularly at the kiosk back into the piece. The unseen border between the natural and urban space, coincidence and intent, micro- and macrocosm is drawn into focus.
John Horton is a sound artist and producer hailing from London and living in Leipzig. Periphery will be audible throughout the exhibition.
